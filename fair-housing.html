Equal Housing Opportunity & Accessibility

Effective August 12, 2025

Our commitment

Sovereign Home Help is committed to fair, lawful, and dignified housing practices. We do not discriminate in any aspect of a housing transaction on the basis of race, color, religion, sex (including pregnancy, sexual orientation, and gender identity), disability, familial status, national origin, age, or any other protected characteristic under federal or state law.

What this means

  • All sellers receive good‑faith consideration and clear, plain English offers.
  • We evaluate properties on merits relevant to the transaction (condition, location, timeline). Never on protected characteristics.
  • We welcome requests for reasonable accommodations related to disability.
  • We partner with title/closing professionals who share these standards.

Accessibility

We aim to meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines. If you experience difficulty using our website or services, please contact us so we can assist and improve.
